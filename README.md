## Key Insights
Based on the analysis of the sales and marketing data, here are the major business findings:

* **Top Product:** **70% Dark Bar** is the star product, generating the highest revenue of **$26.1M**.
* **Regional Performance:** **Australia** is the leading market, contributing **41%** ($42M) of the total revenue.
* **Sales Channels:** **Retail** is the most successful sales channel, bringing in **$53.3M**, while Online channel has room for growth.
* **Top Salesperson:** **Rohan Patel** is the top performer with **$4.9M** in total sales.
* **Marketing Efficiency:** A comparison of monthly marketing spend versus revenue shows stable sales growth, with peak seasons aligned around the end-of-year holidays
